# Witchlight Carnival

A magical traveling carnival that appears with flashing lights and bursts of fireworks. The party encountered it during their return from [[Wytchway]].

## Ownership
The current owners are [[Mr Witch]] and [[Mr Light]], though they are not the original owners and are not from the Feywild. The original owners were the three hags: [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]], [[Scabatha Nightshade]], and [[Endelyn Moongrave]].

## Attractions
- [[Big Top]] - The central tent where major events occur
- [[Lost & Found]] - Watched over by [[Dirlagraun]]
- [[Hall of Illusions]] - Operated by [[Candlefoot]] the mime
- [[Carousel]] - Operated by [[Diana]]
- [[Dragonfly Rides]] - Managed by [[Northwind]] and [[Red]]
- [[Silversong Lake]] - Where a mermaid performs
- [[Mystery Mine]]
- Pixie Kingdom ride
- Snail racing
- Bubble ride at [[Treaclewise]]'s teapot

## Portal to Prismeer
The last mirror in the [[Hall of Illusions]] can become a portal to [[Prismeer]] when the words "Hither, tither, here and there, wonder yonder, show me where" are spoken.
