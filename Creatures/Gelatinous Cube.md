# Gelatinous Cube

A large, translucent ooze creature that was found clogging the pipe leading to [[Bavlorna's Cottage|Bavlorna Blightstraw's]] lair.

## Encounter
[[Sir Morgort]] described to the party how she and her bullywug companions had previously broken into the hag's cottage through an underwater entry. The entrance was guarded by a gelatinous cube blocking the pipe from the pool.

## Bypassing the Cube
The party concocted a plan to lure the cube away with brine, allowing them to swim up the tube and infiltrate [[Bavlorna's Cottage]]. The plan succeeded, giving the party access to the hag's lair from below.

## Location
- [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] (in the underwater pipe entrance)
