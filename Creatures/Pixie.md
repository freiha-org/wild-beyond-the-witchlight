# Pixie

Tiny winged fey creatures known for their magical abilities and mischievous nature.

## Alex Jones
[[Alex Jones]] was a pixie bard who worked as a witchlight hand at the [[Witchlight Carnival]]. He was tasked by [[Mr Light]] to keep an eye on the new guests. Alex Jones later joined the party on their adventures through [[Prismeer]].

He was skilled in persuasion and deception, often blurting out conspiracy theories. He founded the RAF with [[Argon]] during the bullywug revolution and successfully stole [[Mr Witch's Watch]] to delay the carnival.

Alex Jones died during the battle on [[Tin Soldier Mountain]] when his shatter spell failed to cause a rockslide and he was hit by a barrage of javelins.
