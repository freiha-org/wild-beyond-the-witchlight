# Mud Mephit

Small elemental creatures made of mud. They attacked [[Argon]] in [[Hither]] and stole his weapons.

## Encounter
While [[Argon]] was separated from [[Ghee]] during their search for swamp gas, he was attacked by mud mephits who managed to steal his rapier and shortbow. Luckily, [[Ghee]] arrived and dispatched one of them, causing the others to drop the equipment and flee.

## Loot
The party found a weird [[Mud Mephit Heart|heart filled with mud]] in the remains of the mephit's corpse.

## Location
- [[Hither]]
