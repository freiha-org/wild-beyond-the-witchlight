# Merfolk

Aquatic humanoids found swimming in the waters of [[Hither]]. They became more visible after [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]]'s defeat.

## Encounters
- [[Argon]] accidentally stabbed a merfolk while spearfishing
- The party found a tablet describing merfolk fish herding
- After Bavlorna's death, the party spotted merfolk swimming across [[Downfall]]'s lake
- [[Doug]] offered the merfolk a stone, and in return they helped retrieve the bronze frog statue from the bottom of the lake

## Signs of Liberation
After the party defeated [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]], [[Hither]] became greener and happier. One sign of this change was merfolk swimming freely across [[Downfall]]'s lake.

## Locations
- [[Downfall]], [[Hither]]
- Various waters throughout [[Hither]]
