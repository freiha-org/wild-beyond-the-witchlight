# Bullywug

Frog-like humanoids who inhabit the swamps of [[Hither]], particularly the settlement of [[Downfall]].

## Society
After [[Zybilna]]'s fall, the bullywugs formed an aristocratic society with kings, dukes, and barons. They lived under the shadow of [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]], who would occasionally emerge to make demands.

## The Revolution
The party sparked a revolution in [[Downfall]], overthrowing [[King Bullog IX]] with the help of rebellious bullywugs led by [[Baron Illig]]. The revolution succeeded, though the new leadership was more about installing a new monarch than true liberation.

## Notable Bullywugs
- [[King Bullog IX]] - Former ruler of Downfall (beheaded)
- [[Sir Morgort]] - A knight who helped break [[Sir Talavar]] out of Bavlorna's cottage
- [[Wigglewog]] - Brave balloon driver who crashed at the [[Leaning Tower]]
- [[Duke Ickrind]] - Last survivor of the [[Balloon Factory]] fire
- [[Baron Illig]] - Led the revolutionary faction
- [[Alberto]] - A hermit who joined the party and aided in the revolution

## Giant Frog Cavalry
Bullywugs use [[Giant Frog|giant frogs]] as cavalry mounts, keeping them in cages around [[Downfall]].
