# Mimic

A shapeshifting creature that disguises itself as mundane objects to ambush prey. The party encountered one in the [[Leaning Tower]] in [[Hither]].

## Encounter
While exploring the [[Leaning Tower]] waiting for the others to return, [[Ghee]], [[Doug]], and [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]] discovered a mimic on the last floor. They initially decided to ignore it, but later [[Argon]] smacked it, starting a fight.

## The Battle
[[Doug]] was swallowed by the mimic but managed to kill it from the inside.

## Loot
- 400 GP
- A gold necklace worth 60 GP

## Location
- [[Leaning Tower]], [[Hither]]
