# Giant Tadpole

Large tadpoles found in the flooded waters of [[Hither]]. They helped guide the party through the thick fog to [[Downfall]].

## Encounter
When the party needed to reach [[Downfall]] but the land was flooded and the secret water pathway was submerged, [[Ghee]] dove into the water and found giant tadpoles. With [[Leneus]] riding one of them through the water, the tadpoles guided the party through the fog to their destination.

## Location
- [[Hither]] (flooded areas)
