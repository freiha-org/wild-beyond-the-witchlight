# Darkling

Small, shadowy fey creatures sensitive to light. A group of darklings operate a traveling shop near [[Downfall]].

## The Darkling Merchants
[[Trinket]] and [[Bauble]] are darklings who run a shop from a storm cloud-tethered balloon. Their elder, [[Charm]], conducts dealings with [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]].

## The Shop
Their shop sells foreign and common trinkets and baubles, but the prices are unusual - not gold or treasure. [[Trinket]] wants to drain the color of one's eyes, and [[Bauble]] wants to take away the ability to dance. Such are the ways of the Feywild.

## Travel Balloon
Their balloon is used for travel between the three realms of [[Prismeer]]. They transported passengers including a dwarf, an elf, and a tiefling from [[Hither]] to [[Tither]] about three weeks before the party arrived.

## Charm's Betrayal
[[Charm]], a darkling elder, was revealed to be working for [[Scabatha Nightshade]]. She:
- Had no shadow (it was separate from her)
- Orchestrated the lornling attack on the pirate crew
- Attempted to steal Bavlorna's secret chest
- Escaped through the window with the chest during the battle

## Notable Darklings

- [[Bauble]] - Merchant (killed by the black harengon's shockwave)
