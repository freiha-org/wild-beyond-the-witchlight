# Redcap

Malicious fey creatures that serve as officers and overseers in [[Scabatha Nightshade]]'s forces. They command the [[Tin Soldier|tin soldiers]] and supervise the enslaved workers in the [[Tin Soldier Factories]].

## Appearance
Small, gnome-like creatures with red caps soaked in blood, wielding scythes.

## Abilities
- Fight with scythes
- Use basic magic
- Some ride [[Giant Bat|giant bats]]
- Command and coordinate tin soldiers effectively

## Roles
- Lead tin soldier patrols and garrisons
- Oversee slave labor in the factories
- Hunt down escaped prisoners and refugees
- The butcher redcap in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] was grumpy and revealed that the hag mistreated her

## Encounters
- A butcher redcap in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] who revealed Bavlorna's weakness (running in circles causes her to sneeze)
- Multiple redcap lieutenants on [[Tin Soldier Mountain]]
- Redcap overseers at the [[Tin Soldier Factories]]
- Redcaps riding giant bats attacked during the mountain battle

## Notable Weakness
Without redcap leadership, tin soldiers become confused and ineffective in combat.

## Locations
- [[Tither]] (throughout the realm)
- [[Tin Soldier Mountain]]
- [[Tin Soldier Factories]]
- [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] (the butcher)
