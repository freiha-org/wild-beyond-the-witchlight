# Harelope

A rabbit with antelope horns - a magical creature native to [[Prismeer]].

## Encounter
When [[Doug]], [[Leneus]], and [[Dial Up]] fled from the [[Harengon]] bandits in the [[Bandit's Tollway]] and dove into the forest, they found and trailed a harelope. The creature guided them through the forest until they reached a grove where they could rest safely.

## Location
- Forest near the [[Bandit's Tollway]], [[Hither]]
