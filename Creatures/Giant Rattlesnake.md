# Giant Rattlesnake

A massive snake wrapped around a tree stump at the center of the [[Bandit's Tollway]]. It guards the labyrinth alongside an unmanned ballista.

## Abilities
The snake's rattle can deny flying creatures the ability to fly, forcing them to the ground. This makes the [[Bandit's Tollway]] extremely dangerous for aerial travelers.

## Encounter
The party chose to avoid the giant rattlesnake's vision while exploring the tollway, knowing its rattle could send them crashing down. They skirted the edges of the labyrinth rather than confront it directly.

When [[Dial Up]] transformed into a crow and flew into the main corridor, the snake's gaze snapped to him and it shook its rattle, though it ultimately returned to its post after signaling harengon guards.
