# Animated Armor

Magical constructs that materialize from old battlefields. The party encountered them while rowing across an ancient battlefield in [[Hither]].

## Encounter
While rowing their boat across what seemed to be an ancient battlefield, pieces of armor and weapons materialized into animated armors. However, these armors did nothing but sit silently on the boat. Once the party exited the battlefield area, the armors dissipated into dust.

## Behavior
Unlike typical animated armors that attack intruders, these seemed to be echoes of fallen warriors - passive remnants that formed and dissolved based on proximity to the battlefield.

## Location
- Ancient battlefield in [[Hither]]
