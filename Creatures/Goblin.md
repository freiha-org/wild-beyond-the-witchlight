# Goblin

Small, green-skinned humanoids. Several goblins have been encountered throughout the party's adventures.

## Notable Goblins
- [[Nikolas]] - Old goblin ticketmaster at the [[Witchlight Carnival]]
- [[Jingle Jangle]] - A goblin on [[Telemy Hill]] whose clothing is made of keys. She was attacked by harengons who stole her happy memories
- [[Treaclewise]] - A friendly goblin at the carnival who speaks only in rhymes and serves Scatterleaf tea
- [[Snailbrain]] - A goblin pirate found near the [[Balloon Factory]] in [[Downfall]]
- [[Fudd]] (Gurk) - A goblin alchemist and party member
