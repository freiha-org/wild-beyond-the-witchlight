# Kenku

Raven-like humanoids who can mimic sounds and voices. [[Kettlesteam]] is a kenku warlock of [[Zybilna]].

## Kettlesteam
A kenku who had stolen [[Candlefoot]]'s voice. He came to the [[Witchlight Carnival]] seeking answers about why he lost his warlock powers (not knowing [[Zybilna]] was frozen). When the carnival owners revealed nothing, he decided to cause carnage until they confronted him.

He heckled the [[Mermaid]] during her performance at [[Silversong Lake]], causing her to break down in tears. The party chased and interrogated him, eventually coercing him into returning [[Candlefoot]]'s voice.

## Location
- [[Silversong Lake]], [[Witchlight Carnival]]
