# Treant

Ancient tree-like beings with humanoid features. [[Northwind]] is a treant who manages the [[Dragonfly Rides]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]].

## Northwind
A friendly treant with a little red weasel named [[Red]] riding on his shoulder. He revealed to the party that [[Mr Witch]] and [[Mr Light]] aren't the carnival's original owners and aren't even from the Feywild.

## Location
- [[Dragonfly Rides]], [[Witchlight Carnival]]
