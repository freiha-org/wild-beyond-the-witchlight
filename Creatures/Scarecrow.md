# Scarecrow

Animated constructs made of straw, typically created through dark magic. In [[Prismeer]], scarecrows have been used to imprison souls.

## Clapperclaw/Jimmy
The most notable scarecrow encountered was [[Clapperclaw]], who had claws for hands and a gourd for a head (his original head having been stolen by harengons). He spoke with the voice and demeanor of a child.

When the party found his original scarecrow head in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] and placed it on his body, he transformed back into a human boy named [[Jimmy]]. He remembered:
- Traveling with his family
- Being attacked and killed by bandits
- His soul going through the hells before being snatched and put into the scarecrow body

## Obsidian Scarecrow
On [[Tin Soldier Mountain]], the party found a scarecrow at the peak with gems for eyes made of the same dark [[Obsidian Gems|obsidian]] they had found elsewhere. [[Alex Jones]] and [[Argon]] took out the scarecrow and retrieved the gems.
