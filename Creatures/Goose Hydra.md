# Goose Hydra

A monstrous five-headed goose form that [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]] transformed into during the final battle at her cottage.

## The Transformation
When the party nearly killed Bavlorna, she cast polymorph on herself at the last second and transformed into a goose hydra with five heads.

## Abilities
- One attack per head
- Every time a head is cut off, two new ones grow in its place
- The more damage the party dealt to her heads, the more heads she gained

## The Battle
In this form, Bavlorna:
- Spread her attacks across multiple party members
- Commanded her [[Lornling|lornlings]] to detach the ropes holding her cottage to the ground
- Jumped down through a hole in the cottage to reach her healing pool
- Reverted to her regular form once in the pool to rejuvenate

## Location
- [[Bavlorna's Cottage]], [[Hither]]
