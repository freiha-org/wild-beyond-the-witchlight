# Blink Dog

Magical hounds with the ability to blink in and out of reality at will. A pack was encountered by the party in [[Tither]].

## Abilities
- Can teleport short distances ("blink")
- Understand Sylvan but cannot speak it
- Communicate through gestures and body language

## Encounter
The party found a group of blink dogs huddled around a shallow hole, howling around the corpse of one of their dead who had a javelin stuck in its back - evidence of [[Tin Soldier]] attacks.

The dogs used gestures and a drawing of a cross in the dirt to warn the party about the dangers they faced.

## Aftermath
[[Boudia]] spoke to the blink dogs and guided them eastward toward the [[Centaur Village]] for protection.

## Location
- [[Tither]] (near the river, west of the [[Centaur Village]])
