# Dryad

Forest spirits encountered by the party in [[Tither]]. A group of five dryads, led by an angry and bitter leader, ambushed the party near the river's mouth.

## The Ambush
As the party set up camp in the forest, the lead dryad pointed at them and yelled in a gritty, bark-infested voice:

> "Slay the corrupted ones!"

She and the other 4 dryads awakened 9 shrubs to attack the party collectively.

## The Obsidian Bow
The lead dryad wielded the [[Obsidian Bow]], made of shiny black obsidian stone with matching arrowheads. When she hit [[Alex Jones]] with an arrow, it cursed him and withered away part of his life essence.

After defeating the dryads, the party claimed the bow.

## Location
- [[Tither]] (forests near the river's mouth)
