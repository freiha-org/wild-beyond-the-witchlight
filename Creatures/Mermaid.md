# Mermaid

A mermaid performs at [[Silversong Lake]] in the [[Witchlight Carnival]], suspended in a gigantic glass bowl in the middle of the lake.

## The Performance
The mermaid's song is the final event of the carnival evening. Her beautiful, entrancing song has magical properties - when [[Candlefoot]] the mime listened to it, his black and white outfit turned bright and colorful.

## The Incident
During one performance, [[Kettlesteam]] the kenku heckled the mermaid, causing her to break down in tears and retreat into her bowl. This disruption led the party to chase and confront Kettlesteam.
