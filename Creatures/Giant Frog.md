# Giant Frog

Large frogs used as cavalry mounts by the bullywugs of [[Downfall]].

## Role in Downfall
The giant frogs rest in balcony cages and serve as mounts for bullywug soldiers. During the revolution against [[King Bullog IX]], one group of rebellious bullywugs took over the stables and acquired 20 giant frogs to use as cavalry.

## Notable Giant Frog
[[Doug]]'s frog companion, Frederiko, is also a giant frog who has assisted the party on multiple occasions, including:
- Attempting to push [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]] out of her healing pool
- Retrieving the bronze frog statue from the bottom of the lake
