# Giant Bat

Large bats used as mounts by [[Redcap|redcaps]] in [[Scabatha Nightshade]]'s forces.

## Combat Role
Redcaps riding giant bats serve as aerial attackers, swooping down on enemies during ambushes. They attacked the party during the battle on [[Tin Soldier Mountain]].

## Encounter
During the fight at the redcap camp on the mountain, several redcaps on giant bats joined the battle. The party managed to dispatch them, though one redcap escaped on a giant bat.

## Location
- [[Tin Soldier Mountain]], [[Tither]]
