# Weeping Willow

Sentient willow trees found on [[Telemy Hill]] in [[Hither]]. They can move and dance, and occasionally bestow gifts.

## Encounter at Telemy Hill
Five willows greeted [[Argon]] and [[Alex Jones]] when they arrived at [[Telemy Hill]]. The trees showed them the way to [[Jingle Jangle]]'s cave-like abode.

## Dancing Willows
After [[Alex Jones]] spent time with [[Jingle Jangle]] and lifted her spirits, all of the weeping willows on the hill began dancing. Leaves grew on their branches, birds began singing, and crickets chirped - the hill came alive with joy.

## The Key
One dancing willow gave [[Argon]] a special key, speaking a cryptic message:

> "A future door this key fits in, where the hag keeps her kin"

This was the [[Key from the Weeping Willow]].

## Location
- [[Telemy Hill]], [[Hither]]
