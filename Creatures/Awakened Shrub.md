# Awakened Shrub

Plants animated by [[Dryad|dryads]] to serve as their minions.

## Encounter
When the party was ambushed by dryads near the river's mouth in [[Tither]], the lead dryad and her four companions awakened 9 shrubs to attack the party collectively. The shrubs fought alongside the dryads until the party defeated them.

## Location
- [[Tither]] (forests near the river)
