# Tin Soldier

Animated constructs that serve as [[Scabatha Nightshade]]'s army in [[Tither]]. They are created in the [[Tin Soldier Factories]] using fairy dust extracted from enslaved [[Fairy|fairies]].

## Appearance
Mechanical soldiers made of tin, wielding spears and javelins.

## Behavior
- Fight with spears and javelins
- Have trouble operating without [[Redcap]] guidance
- Attack in coordinated groups when led by redcaps
- Become confused and disorganized when their redcap commanders are killed
- Show strange targeting behavior - during one battle they focused solely on [[Argon]], even ignoring attacks from others

## Encounters
The party has encountered tin soldiers throughout [[Tither]], particularly:
- During the ambush on [[Tin Soldier Mountain]]
- At the [[Tin Soldier Factories]]
- In garrisons along mountain passes

## Creation
The tin soldiers are manufactured in factories where enslaved [[Fairy|fairies]] are drained of their fairy dust to animate the constructs.

## Locations
- [[Tither]] (throughout the realm)
- [[Tin Soldier Mountain]]
- [[Tin Soldier Factories]]
