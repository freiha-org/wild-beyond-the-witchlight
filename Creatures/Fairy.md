# Fairy

Small winged fey creatures found throughout [[Prismeer]]. Many have been captured and enslaved by [[Scabatha Nightshade]] in her [[Tin Soldier Factories]].

## Appearance
Normally fairies sparkle with bright colors. However, enslaved fairies have turned purple with a dark obsidian shine due to their captivity and malicious transformation.

## The Enslaved Fairies
Scabatha's forces capture fairies and imprison them in the [[Tin Soldier Factories]], where they are drained of their fairy dust to animate [[Tin Soldier|tin soldiers]]. This prolonged captivity turns the fairies malicious and changes their color to purple.

## Redemption
When the party encountered fairy circles around old withering oak trees in [[Tither]], they found groups of escaped fairies who had turned mischievous and pranking. Through reflection, kind words, and entertainment (including [[Ghee]]'s giant chicken performance), some fairies renounced their mischievous ways and turned bright green again.

## Zhengyi
When [[Ghee]] made [[Owlie]] breathe fire while flying, the fairies excitedly pointed and yelled "Oh it's [[Zhengyi]]! It's [[Zhengyi]]!" - referencing the ancient beast god sleeping in the floating mountains.

## Locations
- [[Tither]] (fairy circles around withering oak trees)
- [[Tin Soldier Factories]] (enslaved)
- [[Floating Mountains]] (referenced)
