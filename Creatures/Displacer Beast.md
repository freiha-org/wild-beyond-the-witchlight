# Displacer Beast

A large, six-legged feline creature with two tentacles sprouting from its shoulders. Known for their ability to project illusions that make them appear to be standing nearby their actual location.

## Known Displacer Beast
[[Dirlagraun]] is a playful displacer beast who watches over the [[Lost & Found]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]]. She speaks only Sylvan and had a cub named [[Star]] who vanished from the carnival.

## Location
- [[Lost & Found]], [[Witchlight Carnival]]
