# Sabertooth Cat

A large feline predator with elongated canine teeth. The party found a friendly one hidden within the [[Bandit's Tollway]].

## Encounter
[[Doug]] and [[Leneus]] discovered an illusory wall inside the tollway labyrinth. When they poked their heads through the other side, they found a sabertooth cat sleeping. Though [[Doug]]'s loud chainmail woke the creature, it proved to be very friendly toward them.

When a horn sounded across the tollway and the [[Harengon]] bandits began searching, the sabertooth cat warned them that "the Harengon are coming," helping them hide in time.

## Location
- [[Bandit's Tollway]], [[Hither]] (hidden chamber)
