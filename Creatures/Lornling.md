# Lornling

Tiny replicas of [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]], these small creatures serve as her minions and spies. Hundreds of them can appear at once, swarming out of lakes and pools throughout [[Hither]].

## Behavior
- Act as [[Bavlorna Blightstraw|Bavlorna's]] eyes and ears across the realm
- Swarm in large numbers when the hag is threatened
- Can detach ropes and perform tasks collectively
- Upon Bavlorna's death, all lornlings inflated and popped into puffs of swamp gas

## Encounters
The party first witnessed lornlings emerging from the lake in [[Downfall]] after the bullywug revolution. They attacked the pirate crew hiding in the Sunken Palace and later aided Bavlorna during the final battle by detaching the ropes holding her cottage to the ground.
