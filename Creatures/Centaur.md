# Centaur

Half-horse, half-humanoid creatures who inhabit [[Tither]]. They have formed a defensive [[Centaur Village]] near the river to protect themselves from [[Scabatha Nightshade]]'s forces.

## The Refugee Village
Hunted to death or slavery by the hag's minions, centaurs fled their former homes and banded together to form a shabby but defensible village. Those who lived in the east had been killed or captured, save for a few survivors.

## Notable Centaurs
- [[Goliolatrix]] - Chief of the village, a black-furred centaur with a golden mane, armed with a decorated longbow and beautiful spear
- [[Boudia]] - A heavily scarred survivor from a village to the northwest that was burned by [[Tin Soldier|tin soldiers]] and [[Redcap|redcaps]]. She joined the party
- [[Diana]] - A melancholic centaur who operates the [[Carousel]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]]

## Culture
- Live in primitive villages with hovels and longhouses
- Build wooden palisades for defense
- Hold feasts to honor friends and share tales
- Skilled with bows and spears

## Boudia's Tale
> "One dark night, whilst I was out hunting, I came back to my village in flames. They were everywhere, the tin soldiers and their redcap lieutenants, with torches, swords and javelins in hand. I barely escaped capture, powering through their blows and projectiles..."
