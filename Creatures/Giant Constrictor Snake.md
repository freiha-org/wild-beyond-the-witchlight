# Giant Constrictor Snake

Two massive serpents that guarded the [[Leaning Tower]] in [[Hither]], where [[Sir Talavar]] and [[Wigglewog]] crashed their balloon.

## Appearance
Each serpent's width is the size of a large door, with lengths matching the tower itself. They move at inhuman speeds through the mud.

## The Two Serpents
1. **Red Serpent** - Has a hidden hood that causes fear when displayed
2. **Green Serpent** - Has a rattle that grounds flying creatures, preventing flight nearby

## Behavior
The serpents wanted to trade [[Sir Talavar]] to [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]] and surrounded the party when they tried to rescue him. They speak in a hissing, drawn-out manner.

## The Battle
The party fought and defeated both serpents after negotiations failed. During the fight:
- [[Alex Jones]] charmed the cobra with soothing music
- [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]] was polymorphed into a sheep
- [[Ghee]] was swallowed whole but freed by [[Argon]]

## Loot
After the battle, the party scavenged:
- Serpent meat
- [[+1 Darts]]
- Serpent teeth
- The hood and scales

## Location
- [[Leaning Tower]], [[Hither]]
