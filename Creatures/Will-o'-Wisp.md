# Will-o'-Wisp

Mysterious floating lights that inhabit O'wells throughout [[Hither]]. They make deals with travelers to exchange trinkets.

## The O'wells
[[Argon]] and [[Ghee]] found O'wells with trinkets at the bottom. Will-o'-wisps lived inside different trinkets in the wells.

## Trinket Trades
The party struck deals with the will-o'-wisps to take various trinkets:
- A tiny wooden unicorn with bright colors
- A weird sundial
- A notebook which seems empty but is not
- A salt shaker shaped like a wizard's tower

## Bavlorna's Cottage
A will-o'-wisp was also found in [[Bavlorna's Cottage|Bavlorna Blightstraw's]] secret room, serving as a light source.
