# Sprite

Tiny fey creatures known for their mischievous nature. A group of four sprites guarded a rotten tree in [[Downfall]].

## Encounter
The party came across a rotten-looking tree with no bark, guarded by 4 rude and uncouth sprites that constantly insulted and taunted them. Most of the party ignored them, but [[Ghee]] made the effort to flip them off.

## Location
- [[Downfall]], [[Hither]]
