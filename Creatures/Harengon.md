# Harengon

Rabbit-like humanoids found throughout [[Prismeer]]. While some are friendly, many have been corrupted by [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]] and turned into bandits and memory thieves.

## Friendly Harengon
- [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]] is a harengon Wild Magic Sorcerer and party member

## The Memory Thieves
In [[Hither]], [[Bavlorna Blightstraw|Bavlorna]] tasked groups of harengon with collecting happy memories from travelers. For every 100 memories collected, she would appear and give them a pet (such as a giant snail or pterodactyl).

The party encountered a group of five harengon, including:
- One riding a giant snail
- Supported by a flying pterodactyl
- Led by a vile, black-furred individual

## The Black Harengon
A particularly dangerous harengon who served as one of Bavlorna's elite agents. This dark-furred harengon:
- Led the memory-stealing bandits who attacked [[Jingle Jangle]]
- Hid under Bavlorna's bed in her cottage
- Could create shockwaves that instantly killed [[Trinket]] and [[Bauble]]
- Was ultimately slain by the party during the battle in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]]

## The Bandit's Tollway
Harengon bandits controlled the [[Bandit's Tollway]], a dangerous labyrinth guarded by a giant rattlesnake. They patrolled the walls in pairs and set arrow traps for intruders.
