# Bugbear

Large, hairy goblinoids. [[Burly]] is a bugbear who works as carnival staff at the [[Witchlight Carnival]].

## Burly
[[Burly]] caught the party when they tried to enter the staff area and investigate [[Mr Witch]] and [[Mr Light]]'s wagon. He privately told [[Alex Jones]] about his brother being lost, and asked him to try to delay the carnival by a day so Burly could search for him. He revealed the only way to do this was to take [[Mr Witch]]'s watch or [[Mr Light]]'s vane.

## Location
- [[Witchlight Carnival]] (staff area)
