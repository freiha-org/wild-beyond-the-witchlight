Wigglit (also known by various nicknames: Wiglit, Wigglefuzz Quibblefur, Quibblit, Wigglefur, Wiggle, Weegwoo, Wargrat, Wiggywog) is a Harengon Sorcerer from the Feywild. Not knowing how or why he transformed from a rabbit to a Harengon, he has traveled the lands in look for an answer!

Wigglit comes off as naive and nonchalant, but wields terrific sorcery using his wild magic!

## Items
