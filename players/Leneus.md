Leneus (nicknamed Lenny) is a Satyr druid from the Feywild. They are heavily connected with nature, always striving to perfect his concoctions and remedies, and wielding well his druidic magic and beastly powers.

He is a hermit, finding no kinship among the druids nor among the Satyrs.

## Abilities
- Can cast speak with animals
- Learned to turn into different animals from [[Tsu Harabax]]
- Can turn into a spider
- Can commune with the forest

## Notable Events
- Communicated with the giant serpents at the [[Leaning Tower]]
- Rode a giant tadpole to find the path to [[Downfall]]
- Explored the [[Bandit's Tollway]] with [[Doug]] and [[Dial Up]]
- Froze and started T-posing during the serpent encounter
