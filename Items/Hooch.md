# Hooch

**Effect:** Explodes upon being thrown, dealing 3d6 fire damage in a 2m diameter on impact.

**Quantity:** 6 bottles taken

**Owners:**
- [[Ghee]] - 3 bottles
- [[Alex Jones]] - 3 bottles

**Found at:** Distillery room (B17) in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]]
