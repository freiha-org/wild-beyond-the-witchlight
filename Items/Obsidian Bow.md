# Obsidian Bow

A bow made of shiny black obsidian stone, with arrows of similar material.

**Effect:** Curses the target and withers away part of their life essence.

**Former Owner:** Lead Dryad (slain)
**Current Owner:** Party - taken after defeating the dryads in [[Tither]]

[[Alex Jones]] was hit by one of its arrows, which cursed him and withered part of his life essence.
