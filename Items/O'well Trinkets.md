# O'well Trinkets

A collection of trinkets found at the bottom of O'wells in [[Hither]], guarded by Will O'Wisps.

**Items:**
- A tiny wooden unicorn with bright colors
- A weird sundial
- A notebook which seems empty but is not
- A salt shaker shaped like a wizard's tower

**Found by:** [[Ghee]] and [[Argon]] - struck a deal with the Will O'Wisps to take the trinkets
