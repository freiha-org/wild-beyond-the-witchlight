# Spells Used

A record of spells cast during the campaign.

## Party Spells

### [[Leneus]]
- **Speak with Animals** - Used to communicate with the giant serpents at the [[Leaning Tower]]
- **Wild Shape** - Transforms into different animals (learned more forms from [[Tsu Harabax]])
  - Spider form (used to explore wells in [[Bandit's Tollway]])
- **Commune with Nature** - Used to sense the forest's fear

### [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]]
- **Chaos Bolt** - Wild Magic sorcery; triggers random effects
  - Cast on serpent at [[Leaning Tower]], starting combat
  - Cast on Redcap at [[Tin Soldier Mountain]], triggered growth to Large size

### [[Alex Jones]]
- **Shatter** - Sonic damage spell
  - Used to destroy harengon nest at tollway tower
  - Attempted rockslide on [[Tin Soldier Mountain]] (failed, Alex died)
  - Broke the floor of [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]]'s pool

### [[Ghee]]
- **Summon Familiar** ([[Owlie]]) - Can be resummoned in different forms including giant chicken

### [[Dial Up]]
- **Invisibility** - Used for scouting
- **Shapeshifting** - Transforms into crow and hybrid forms

## Enemy Spells

### [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]]
- **Magic Missile** - Cast 12 missiles on [[Alberto]]
- **Polymorph** - Turned herself into a goose hydra with five heads
- **Mouth Sewing** - Sewed [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]]'s mouth shut

### [[Tasha]] (Statue)
- **Hideous Laughter** - Enchanted statue at [[Witchlight Carnival]] causes crying

## Spells Cast on Party
- **Polymorph** - [[Wigglit Quibblefuzz]] turned into a sheep during serpent battle
