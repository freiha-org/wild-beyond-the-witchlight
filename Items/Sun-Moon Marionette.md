# Sun-Moon Marionette

A 12-inch tall wooden marionette with two faces on opposite sides of its head.

**Description:**
- One face, clad in silver, looks like the moon
- The other face, clad in gold, looks like the sun
- Both sides connect to the wooden marionette with obsidian

**Found by:** [[Alberto]] - in the locked chest in [[Bavlorna's Cottage]] after the hag's defeat
