# Dial Up

A member of the adventuring party. He participated in goblin wrestling with [[Alex Jones]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]].

Dial Up has shapeshifting abilities, being able to transform into a crow and a hybrid form. He can also turn invisible. He helped scout the [[Bandit's Tollway]] and later [[Tin Soldier Mountain]], using his powers of invisibility to speak with [[Gib]].
