# King Bullog IX

The Bullywug king who rules from his [[Soggy Court]] in [[Downfall]]. He welcomed the party and gave them accommodation inside the [[Balloon Factory]].

King Bullog IX arrested and killed a party of bullywugs that caused a fire at the [[Balloon Factory]] and stole an important prisoner ([[Sir Talavar]]) from [[Bavlorna's Cottage|Bavlorna Blightstraw's lair]]. The sole survivor, [[Sir Morgort]], was imprisoned and scheduled for trial by combat.

The king was overthrown during a Bullywug revolution led by [[Baron Illig]]'s forces and the party. He was beheaded using a makeshift wooden guillotine built by the revolutionaries.
