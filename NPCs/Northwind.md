# Northwind

A treant who manages the [[Dragonfly Rides|Dragonfly racing ride]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]]. He has a little red weasel named [[Red]] riding on his shoulder.

Northwind revealed to the party that [[Mr Witch]] and [[Mr Light]] aren't the carnival's original owners and aren't even from the Feywild.
